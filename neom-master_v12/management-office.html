STRATEGY MANAGEMENT OFFICE (SMO)

Home Strategy Management Office

About the Division

The cybersecurity strategy management office (SMO) is a function responsible for acting as the central point of contact for NEOM’s cybersecurity strategy. The function will also be expected to support strategic and technical discussions linked to requirements or changes of NEOM’s cybersecurity strategy and standards. As part of its remit, the cybersecurity SMO function would align cybersecurity objectives (REALISED), guiding principles and operating model with NEOM’s business model.

Key Activities

STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT
DEMAND PRIORITIZATION
TRAINING & AWARENESS
PROCESS OPTIMIZATION

Ensure alignment and support from sectoral stakeholders and key decision makers for the cybersecurity strategy

Identify overlaps across projects and develop mitigation plans involving various functional leads within CISO Office

Manage the demand prioritization for cybersecurity across sectors, businesses and departments based on key criteria (e.g., business benefits, cost benefits and process criticality) to achieve targeted cybersecurity strategic objectives

Manage the communication of cybersecurity goals and strategy across NEOM

Manage the awareness of cybersecurity through various channels in NEOM – to share success stories, drive adoption and build comfort over the use of solutions across NEOM

Advise on re-engineering of processes wherever necessary to reduce identified strategic cyber risks

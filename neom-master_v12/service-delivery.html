About the division

The Cybersecurity Service Delivery division is responsible for overseeing the cybersecurity portfolio/project implementation while aligning project team efforts towards achieving departmental objectives. It acts as the single point of contact for Department’s projects and initiatives, and monitors the project implementation through the form of key performance indicators (KPIs) and dashboards.

Key Activities

DILIVERY AND GOVERNANCE
BUSINESS OPERATIONS
  • 1.Forecast efficient alignment with internal and external stakeholders including other NEOM sectors, partners, customers, and government entities where needed.
  • 2.Support strategy alignment and execution by ensuring projects in themselves and overall are aligned with the department’s and NEOM’s 2030 vision and annual strategic targets.
  • 1.Manage the cybersecurity department annual budgets and monthly accrual forecasts through an effective financial planning and management of expenditure against budgets by ensuring the accuracy of CAPEX and OPEX expenditure
  • 2.Streamline the business demand process from Initiation/Ideation stage, procurement stages until the awarding
